APSA @ Rice

The American Physician Scientists Association (APSA) is an NIH-funded and student-led 501C3 non-profit organization representing over 1000 physician-scientist trainees.

The national organization was founded by Rice Alum Freddy T. Nguyen, M.D./Ph.D.

Our local chapter is dedicated to addressing the needs of future physician-scientists at Rice, including helping Rice Pre-Medical Students who are applying to M.D./Ph.D. programs or generally interested in conducting research during medical school.

Our Vision

Connect

We want to support a network of Rice students and alumni to foster communication between individuals with similar interests and provide access to research and other opportunities.

Mentor

We want to provide high quality resources for Rice Pre-Medical students interested in pursuing an M.D./Ph.D. or research-track medical career after graduation.

Inspire

We want to see more Rice students engaging with medical and translational research, and facilitate opportunities for students to present their work at regional and national venues.
